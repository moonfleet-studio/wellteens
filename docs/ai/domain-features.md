# WellTeen Domain & Feature Notes

- Audience: teenagers seeking mental health support; tone should be empathetic, inclusive, and age-appropriate.
- Platforms: Expo-managed workflow targeting iOS, Android, and web.
- Core features (MVP):
  - Authentication: allow teens to create an account and sign in securely.
  - Mood tracking: users can set and review their current mood and add notes.
  - Articles: curated written resources focused on teen mental wellness.
  - Videos: short-form video content with coping strategies and education.
  - Modules: combined sets of articles and videos organized by topic for deeper exploration.
- Future considerations:
  - Safe-guard mechanisms, e.g., crisis support links and parental guidance info.
